# Privacy Policy for Content Management Tool

**Last updated:** 2026-04-22

Content Management Tool ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") is used only by the developer to manage and publish content to Facebook Pages that they own or have authorized access to.

## Scope of Use

This App is intended for personal/internal use only.

It is not offered as a public service or platform for third-party users.

## 1. What this App does

The App only helps authorized users:

- sign in with Facebook
- view Facebook Pages they manage
- select Pages they have permission to manage
- publish posts to those Pages

The App is not intended for unrelated data collection, advertising profiling, or resale of user data.

## 2. Information we may receive

When you use the App with Facebook Login and Page permissions, we may receive limited information such as:

- your Facebook user ID
- basic profile information made available by Meta during login
- the list of Facebook Pages you manage
- Page IDs, Page names, and granted permissions
- Page access tokens required to publish content
- post content that you choose to send through the App
- technical logs required for security, debugging, and service reliability

## 3. How we use information

We use the information only to:

- authenticate your account with Facebook Login
- identify the Facebook Pages you are authorized to manage
- publish posts to Facebook Pages at your request
- maintain the functionality, security, and reliability of the App
- investigate technical issues and prevent abuse
- comply with legal obligations where required

## 4. What we do not do

We do not:

- sell your personal data
- use your Facebook data for advertising profiling
- access Pages that you do not manage or do not authorize
- publish content without your action or authorization

## 5. Legal basis and authorization

The App accesses Facebook and Page-related data only after you explicitly authorize the App through Meta's login and permission flow.

## 6. Data sharing

We do not share Facebook-derived data with third parties except in the following limited cases:

- with service providers acting on our behalf for hosting, infrastructure, logging, or security
- when required by law, regulation, court order, or legal process
- when necessary to protect the security, integrity, rights, or operation of the App

## 7. Data retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to operate the App and provide the posting functionality requested by the user.

This may include retaining limited records such as:

- Facebook user identifiers associated with the App
- Page identifiers and permission records
- access tokens for active sessions or integrations
- post history, drafts, or logs required for troubleshooting and security

If you disconnect the App, request deletion, or stop using the App, we will delete or anonymize stored data within a reasonable period unless we are legally required to retain certain records longer.

## 8. Data security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no method of storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

## 9. Your choices and rights

You may:

- stop using the App at any time
- remove the App from your Facebook account settings
- request deletion of your App-related data

Please see our data deletion instructions here:

[User Data Deletion Instructions](https://manhlh82.github.io/app-policy-public/cmt-deletion)

## 10. Facebook Platform data

The App uses Meta platform data only for the features described in this policy and only within the scope of permissions granted by the user.

## 11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
