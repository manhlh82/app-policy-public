# User Data Deletion Instructions for Content Management Tool

**Last updated:** 2026-04-22

If you would like your data associated with Content Management Tool ("the App") to be deleted, please follow one of the methods below.

## Method 1: Remove the App from Facebook

You can remove the App from your Facebook account by following these steps:

1. Log in to your Facebook account.
2. Go to **Settings & privacy**.
3. Open **Settings**.
4. Go to **Apps and Websites**.
5. Find **Content Management Tool**.
6. Click **Remove**.

Removing the App will revoke the App's access to your Facebook data.

## Method 2: Request deletion by email

You may also request deletion directly by contacting:

**cafeshot@gmail.com**

Please include:

- the email address you want us to use to contact you
- your Facebook Page name or names, if relevant
- a short statement that you want your App-related data deleted

## What data may be deleted

Upon a valid request, we will delete or anonymize, where applicable:

- stored Facebook user identifiers associated with the App
- stored Page identifiers and related access records
- stored Page access tokens
- stored post drafts or posting records that are no longer required
- logs and related technical records that are no longer needed for security or compliance

## Retention exceptions

We may retain limited information if required for:

- legal compliance
- fraud prevention
- dispute resolution
- security and abuse prevention

## Processing time

We aim to process valid deletion requests within **30 days** of receiving and verifying the request.
